┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                          CSV OUTPUT FORMAT                                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

   Each COMPANY gets multiple rows (one per EXECUTIVE):

   Row 1: Company A │ Executive 1 │ Email 1 │ Phone 1 │ LinkedIn 1
   Row 2: Company A │ Executive 2 │ Email 2 │ Phone 2 │ LinkedIn 2
   Row 3: Company A │ Executive 3 │ Email 3 │ Phone 3 │ LinkedIn 3
   Row 4: Company B │ Executive 1 │ Email 1 │ Phone 1 │ LinkedIn 1
   ...

   ✨ This allows you to:
      • Contact multiple people at each company
      • Import all contacts into CRM at once
      • Sort/filter by executive title in Excel


┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                         EXAMPLE USE CASE                                    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

   Sales Team Workflow:

   1. Marketing identifies 50 target companies
      └─▶ Creates CSV: target_companies.csv

   2. Upload to LeadStorefrontAI
      └─▶ Bulk enrichment via SeamlessAI
      └─▶ Gets 250+ executive contacts (5 per company average)

   3. Download enriched CSV
      └─▶ enriched_targets_2025-01-09.csv

   4. Import to Salesforce
      └─▶ 50 companies + 250 contacts
      └─▶ Ready for outreach!

   5. Sales team starts calling/emailing
      └─▶ Using real, verified contact info
      └─▶ Higher connection rates!
